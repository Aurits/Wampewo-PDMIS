Our Mission

At Wampeewo Parish, our mission is to create a sustainable and inclusive community where all residents have access to opportunities, resources, and services that improve their quality of life. Through collaborative efforts, we aim to uplift individuals from the subsistence economy, foster economic growth, and promote social well-being.

Our Vision

Our vision is to transform Wampeewo Parish into a model of prosperity, resilience, and shared success. We envision a future where every member of the community is empowered to participate in economic activities, access essential services, and contribute to the overall development of the parish. Through the Parish Development Model, we aim to break the cycle of poverty and create a thriving and united community.
